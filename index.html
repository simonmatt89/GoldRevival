About

Gold Revival is a live function band from Birmingham, formed by members Jodi (vocals), Simon (guitar), Paul (drums) and kate (bass guitar). Gold Revivial is availble for weddings, private parties, and corporate events across the West Midlands. Gold Revival is a dynamic four-piece band that boasts a wide range of influences and a repertoire that spans several decades. With their energetic stage presence, polished performances, and dedication to customer satisfaction, Gold Revival has established themselves as one of the premier function bands in the region.
